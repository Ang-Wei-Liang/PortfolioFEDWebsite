Career Prospects

So, what do I want to pursue in future?

Introduction

According to this Holland Code Career Test by Truity, My top interest areas are organising, persuading and creating, with the scores of 100, 89, 80 respectively. I am organised and have attention to detail. I enjoy following procedures and working with other people. I find excitement in knowing that there is task to be accomplished, and in working hard for it. I often ponder about what the future can be with information tecnhology, and believe in its power to change the world for the better.

Career Choices

Data Adminstrator

Being a data administrator involves the designing and maintaining of databases, and the governing of data access.

I would be suited for this as I am reliable and will be able to complete this task with accuracy and precision.

Full-Stack Developer

Being a full-stack web developer involves developing and maintaining websites, both on the front-end and the back-end.

I would have the creative freedom to design webpage elements and keep the databases organised. I will be able to communicate with the management to find out their requirements and do my best to fulfil them.

Software Developer

Being a software engineer involves the creating, developing and implementing of software for industrial use.

I enjoy programming and this job will allow me to use my knowledge and skills and >solve problems creatively. I like working in a team to solve such problems and when we eventually do, I will gain a huge sense of accomplishment.

My Choice

If I had to choose a career out of these 3, it will definitely be being a software engineer. Not only is it highly sought after and highly paid, it involves solving world real problems which excites me.
