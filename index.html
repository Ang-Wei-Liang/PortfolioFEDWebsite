Introduction

I am Ang Wei Liang, a full-time student in the Singapore Polytechnic Diploma in Information Technology course. I am in class 3. I aim to achieve the best version of myself and my dream is to develop an application that can change the world.

Interests

I like to listen to music on my bed, because I often overthink and music helps me to put these thoughts aside. I really appreciate music because it relates to the emotions I am feeling and reminds me of my nostalgic past. Everyone has a shared experience of the song and their own interpretation of it.

For example, the theme song for the japanese trading card game "buddyfight" reminds me of the time my brother and I would catch the latest episode, buy the cards and play the game. Sadly, he is now more interested in using his phone and we do not spend much time together anymore.

I also like to play games such as brawl stars and watch esports, as they are entertaining and bring me excitement.

My Past

I was from Chua Chu Kang Secondary School where I learnt to serve, grow and excel. I was a stressed mugger because grades were a priority to me, and my efforts paid off as I managed to score well.

My Co-Curricular activity was Saint John Brigade where I learnt foot-drills and first aid. Examples of injuries included fractures, sprains and burns. As a uniformed group, we were often shouted at by the officers and while it was stressful, it played an integral role in shaping me to become more disciplined and resilient.

My family and I went to Genting Highlands Malaysia at the end of 2019. We explored the hotel there which was the world's largest.

Personality

I have the ISTJ personality type, also known as the logistician. Because I was brought up under strict parents who restricted how I spent my time and money, I did not have a desire to buy anything that was not needed, such as a movie ticket or a keychain.

I became purpose driven, in which I would only act according to what I should do.

Because of this, I found it hard to make friends, because I have no desire to create small talk and find out more about them. I work best in group projects where there is an objective to be completed, and hence a reason for me to communicate with the team.
